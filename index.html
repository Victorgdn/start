Our concept

We give courses to older persons which have difficulties using computer, tablets and phones in one to one. We can do it via zoom or in face-to face depending your availabilities;flexibility is our main value. Our professors are kind young people and good instructor. In few hours, we can teach good reaction to our student on Internet.

We aim to make life easier for seniors: they will be able to communicate better with their family, shop online, buy a plane ticket or watch Netflix. Our courses are based on exchange and listening.
